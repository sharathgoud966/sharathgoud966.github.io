Process
BEHIND
THE DESIGN

Every poster begins as an idea — a mood, a message, or a feeling. Here's how the process unfolds from concept to final print-ready file.

01
CONCEPT & MOODBOARD
Defining the visual language — color story, typographic direction, compositional intention.
02
SKETCH & LAYOUT
Rough layouts exploring grid structure, hierarchy, and negative space before touching software.
03
DESIGN & REFINE
Executed in Illustrator and Photoshop — typography set, colors balanced, composition locked.
04
PRINT PREP
CMYK conversion, bleed setup, color proofing — print-ready at 300dpi minimum.
